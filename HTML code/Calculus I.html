__ Life is like Calculus. You just need to limit your spirits to infinity __



















As you can see above, I achieve a score of 9.0 out of 10.0 in Calculus I, which seems to be a pretty high score compared to other peers. The secret behind my success in this subject all boils down to my preparation in materials and thorough revision, together with a bit of luck, for sure =)). Anyway, as a former HUST student having experience in dealing with Calculus I, I wanna share with you some of my materials, including lectures and test banks which will probably come in handy for freshmen. By completing these exercises I have been able to consolidate my knowledge as well as gaining confidence in my final exam. Thanks to this score, I got an A in Calculus I and finally obtaining a scholarship in my first semester (^_^)


However, don't be disappointed about yourself if you cannot finish the test to the best of your ability, since Calculus I is a difficult subject. Make sure that you have revised as thoroughly as you can and tried your best in the exam, then there's nothing to have regrets about. If you're still feeling afraid of failing the exam or having difficulty in this subject, I reassure you that this is a normal thing and you have to accept it. Especially in Bach Khoa HN, you will face more challenging problems in studying than in most of the other universities; therefore, if you're not a HUST-er, don't put too much pressure on yourself and even if you only get an average score of 5 or 6 out of 10, cheer yourself up because you are just in the same boat with many other students :))
